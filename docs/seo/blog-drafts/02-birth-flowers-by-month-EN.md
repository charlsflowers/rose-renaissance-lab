## Body (markdown)

Just like a birthstone, every month has its own **birth flower** — a bloom traditionally tied to the people born in it. It makes one of the most thoughtful, personal gifts you can send. Here's the complete month-by-month guide.

## What is a birth flower?
A birth flower is the flower historically associated with each calendar month. Sending someone their birth flower (or a bouquet in its colors) turns an ordinary bouquet into something personal — "I picked this because it's *yours*."

## Can't find their birth flower? Send roses in its colors
Not every birth flower is in season year-round — but roses always are. If the exact birth flower isn't available, the easiest move is a rose bouquet in the birth flower's color: warm **yellow roses** for a summer birthday, soft **pink roses** for spring, classic **red roses** for romance, elegant **white roses** for purity.

## Send a birthday bouquet the same day in Miami
Every bouquet is hand-arranged in Miami and delivered the same day up to 87 miles, with FedEx shipping beyond that and free store pickup in Miami, FL. Browse birthday flowers and pick a color that matches the birth month.
