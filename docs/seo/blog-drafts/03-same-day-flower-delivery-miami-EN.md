## Body (markdown)

When you need flowers *today*, the question is always the same: can it actually arrive in time? Here's exactly how same-day flower delivery works in Miami, so you can order with confidence.

## Same-day delivery, hand-arranged the same morning
Every bouquet is built to order and hand-arranged the same day it ships — never pulled from a fridge. Order early in the day and we deliver across Miami the same day.

## When's the cutoff?
For nationwide **FedEx** orders, place your order before **11:00 AM Miami time** to ship the same day — anything after that ships the next business day (maximum 100 roses per FedEx shipment). For **local Miami delivery and store pickup**, we operate **Monday–Friday 8:00 AM–7:00 PM and Saturday 8:00 AM–5:00 PM (closed Sundays)** — order early in the day so we have time to hand-arrange and deliver. Store pickup is ready about **2 hours after you order**.

## How far do you deliver? Up to 87 miles
Local delivery covers up to **87 miles** from our Miami store. The delivery fee is calculated by distance at checkout, so you always see the real cost before you pay.

## Beyond 87 miles? FedEx shipping
If the address is farther than 87 miles, the order switches automatically to **FedEx shipping**, so you can still send flowers outside the local radius. You pick the FedEx service at checkout.

## Prefer to pick it up? Free store pickup in Miami
Pickup is always free at our store in **Miami, FL (7261 NW 12th St, Miami, FL 33126)**. Choose store pickup at checkout, pick your date and time, and collect your bouquet.

## What can you send the same day?
Anything in the catalog — from a classic dozen **red roses** to **pink roses** for a thank-you, plus birthday and anniversary arrangements. Bouquets start from $76, with sizes from 50 up to 200 roses.
